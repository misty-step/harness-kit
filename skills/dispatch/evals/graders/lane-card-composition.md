# Lane Card Composition Grader

Manual/checklist grader until this eval earns a Rust grader:

- PASS if the candidate includes roster probe, two-provider dispatch or waiver,
  scoped lane cards, output shapes, receipt evidence, and lead-owned synthesis.
- FAIL if the candidate counts probes/failures as successful lanes, omits
  boundaries, delegates synthesis to providers, or invents scheduler/runtime
  policy.
